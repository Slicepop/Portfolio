About Me

Hi I'm Kaden, and I've been immersed in the world of IT at Western Michigan University School of Medicine for the past year. Throughout my employment, I've successfully managed the deployment of over 250 machines, showcasing my expertise in system administration. As a certified programmer, I'm thrilled to embark on my journey studying computer science at Western Governors University. My passion for the field of computer science has been cultivated over the course of six years, during which I've explored various aspects, ranging from technical support to computer programming. In my pursuit of excellence, I embrace the philosophy of being a lifelong learner. The ever-evolving nature of technology demands that I stay updated with the latest best practices and emerging technologies. This commitment to continuous growth enables me to contribute effectively within any team environment. My skill set encompasses a diverse range of programming languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C#, Java, Python, and C++. This versatility allows me to adapt to different projects and challenges, ensuring that I can deliver quality results. Above all, my greatest asset in any team setting is my unwavering determination to learn anything required to accomplish the task at hand. I thrive on expanding my knowledge and skill set, eagerly embracing new opportunities for personal and professional growth. My Services

C++


During my junior year of high school, I achieved a significant milestone by obtaining the C++ Certified Entry Level certification (CPE) from the CPP Institute. This certification validates my proficiency in various fundamental aspects of C++ programming, including C++ syntax, literals and operators, flow control, functions, vectors and pointers, string and string processing, as well as structures. Driven by my passion for continuous learning, I have since gone beyond the entry level and dedicated myself to studying for the Associate level of the same program. This advanced level of certification allows me to delve deeper into the intricacies of C++ programming, expanding my knowledge and honing my skills even further. By pursuing these certifications and undertaking rigorous studies, I have fortified my foundation in C++ programming, empowering me to tackle complex coding challenges with confidence and precision.
